Social Media Checklist

✔ Do’s:

-Use professional and respectful language
-Keep privacy settings enabled 
-Think before posting content 
-Verify information before sharing 
-Maintain a positive digital footprint

❌ Don’ts:

-Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
-Do not engage in cyberbullying or harmful comments 
-Do not click on suspicious links
-Do not accept unknown friend/follow requests blindly
-Do not post inappropriate or offensive content
