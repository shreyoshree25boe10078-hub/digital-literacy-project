Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Do not share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
2. Always check the website URL before entering personal information.
3. Do not click on suspicious links in emails or messages.
4. Use strong passwords and change them regularly.
5. Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts.
6. Never use public Wi-Fi for online banking or payments.
7. For UPI safety, always verify the receiver’s name before sending money.
8. For UPI safety, do not approve collect requests from unknown people.
9. Report cybercrime at https://cybercrime.gov.in
10. Call helpline number 1930 to report financial cyber fraud.
